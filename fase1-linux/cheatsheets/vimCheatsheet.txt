gg -> go to the top of the document
G  -> go to the end of the document
/text -> search for "text"
?text -> search backwards for "text"
^  -> move to start of a line
$  -> move to end of a line
w  -> move to the next word
e  -> move to the end of the current word
:%s/old/new/g -> substitute all occurrences of "old" with "new"
b  -> go back to the start of the previous word
"+y -> copy to out of vim (clipboard)
